Why LED Screens

Why Advertise On The Screens

  • Unmatched exposure on the Croisette during peak event traffic.
  • Near-total reach: your message is seen by almost every attendee.
  • A premium format to deliver powerful visuals and strong brand storytelling.
  • Full flexibility to spotlight a key launch, product, or campaign during the event.
  • Position your brand alongside industry leaders, strengthening credibility and perception.
Dynamic Visibility, Day And Night

Advantages Of LED Screens

  • Fully animated content for maximum visual impact
  • Extremely bright, ensuring excellent visibility day and night
  • Highly eye-catching, naturally drawing attention in busy environments
  • Limited number of advertisers per screen, reducing clutter and increasing recall
  • A powerful marketing tool to enhance brand presence and awareness
  • Ability to alternate between multiple videos within a rotation
